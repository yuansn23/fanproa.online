Our Programs

Hope Home Initiative

Providing essential support to uplift families from poverty through comprehensive assistance programs.

  • Monthly living allowance for poor families (including food and education subsidies)
  • Housing renovation special fund
  • Clean drinking water project coverage
  • Vocational skills connection services

Women Empowerment Plan

Enabling women to achieve financial independence through skills development and health support.

  • Vocational skills training (textiles, e-commerce, handicrafts)
  • Maternal and child health support package

Special Care Program

Focused support for vulnerable populations with specialized needs and development opportunities.

  • Employment training for persons with disabilities
  • Nutritional support for school children
  • Youth skills scholarship program
